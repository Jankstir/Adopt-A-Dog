About Us

Welcome to Adopt a Dog! We are a non-profit, no-kill animal adoption shelter dedicated to providing a safe haven for abandoned, lost, and surrendered animals. Our mission is to find loving, forever homes for every one of our furry friends, while also promoting responsible pet ownership, community education, and animal welfare.

Founded in 2023, our shelter has become a beacon of hope for countless animals and pet lovers in the Saint Paul area. Our dedicated team of passionate staff and volunteers work tirelessly to provide a clean, comfortable, and loving environment for all our residents.

At Adopt a Dog, we believe that every animal deserves a second chance at a happy life. We strive to make this possible by providing comprehensive care, medical attention, and behavioral training to ensure that each pet is ready for adoption. Our team works closely with prospective adopters to find the perfect match for their home and lifestyle.
